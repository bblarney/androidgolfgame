## Full description (max 4000 chars)
```
Greenside is a pick-up-and-play 3D golf game built for one-handed phone sessions.

Pull back, aim, and let go — the slingshot drag-to-shoot controls take a second to learn and a
round to master. Every hole is procedurally generated, so the course is never the same twice:
rolling fairways, water and sand hazards, tricky greens, and shifting wind across changing
biomes.

Sink putts to earn points, then spend them in the Pro Shop on randomized clubs and balls — each
with its own stats and rarity. Build your perfect loadout in the Clubhouse and take it into
practice rounds, 9-hole runs, 18-hole rounds, and tournaments.

FEATURES
• Simple, satisfying drag-to-shoot controls — fully playable with one thumb
• Endless procedurally generated holes — a fresh course every round
• Randomized loot: collect clubs and balls of varying rarity from the Pro Shop
• Customize your bag in the Clubhouse
• Practice, 9-hole, 18-hole, and tournament modes
• Dynamic wind, multiple biomes, and 3D terrain
• Career stats that track your best rounds
• 100% offline — no ads, no in-app purchases, no account, no data collection

Play a quick round on the bus or chase a tournament streak at home. Grab your putter and go.
```

## Screenshot shot-list
Capture these on a real phone or in the editor at phone resolution (portrait):
1. **Mid-shot** — ball at address with the aim dart/power arc drawn (the core mechanic).
2. **Hero hole view** — a scenic hole with flag, hazard, and a distinct biome.
3. **Pro Shop** — the loot grid showing club/ball rarity.
4. **Clubhouse** — loadout management screen.
5. **Scorecard / stats** — a completed round or the career stats screen.
6. **Tournament hub** — modes / progression.

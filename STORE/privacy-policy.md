# Privacy Policy — Birdie Blitz

_Last updated: 2026-06-29_

Birdie Blitz ("the app", "we") is a single-player, offline golf game. Your privacy matters, so
this policy is short because the app does very little with data.

## Information we collect

**None.** Birdie Blitz does **not** collect, transmit, or share any personal information. The app
has no user accounts, no login, and no analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.

## Data stored on your device

The app saves your game progress — points, inventory, loadout, career stats, and any in-progress
round — to a local file in the app's private storage on your device
(`save.json`). This data:

- never leaves your device,
- is not accessible to us or any third party,
- is removed when you uninstall the app or clear its data.

## Permissions

Birdie Blitz requests **no special Android permissions**. It does not access the internet,
location, camera, microphone, contacts, or files outside its own private storage.

## Children's privacy

Because the app collects no data, it is suitable for all ages and complies with policies for
children's apps. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone.

## Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at the same URL with a new "Last
updated" date.
